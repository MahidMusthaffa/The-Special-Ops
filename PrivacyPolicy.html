Privacy Policy - The Special Ops

At Lost Production, protecting your privacy is our priority.

1. Data We Collect

  • Gameplay Data: Levels completed, achievements, and preferences.
  • Device Information: Model, OS, and performance statistics.
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous data to fix bugs and improve gameplay.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies to enhance your user experience.

2. Why We Collect Your Data

  • To optimize game performance for your device.
  • To enhance gameplay features and save progress.
  • To improve overall game quality by identifying and fixing bugs.
  • To provide personalized content and better recommendations.

3. Advertising Policy

We do not currently advertise in the game. However, in the future, ads may be integrated to support the game. Updates to this policy will be provided when changes occur.

Any ads shown in the future will comply with the highest standards of user privacy and will be transparent about data usage.

4. Third-Party Services

We may collaborate with trusted services for analytics or cloud storage. These services adhere to strict privacy standards.

Some third-party providers may use cookies or similar technologies to collect data, but we only share necessary data to improve the game.

5. Security Measures

We implement encryption and regular audits to ensure your data is protected at all times. We also ensure secure data transmission during online interactions within the game.

While we take every precaution, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Multiplayer Features

The Special Ops includes multiplayer features allowing players to connect and play online. Any data shared during multiplayer sessions is limited to gameplay purposes and handled securely.

We do not share personal data without your explicit consent. Multiplayer interactions may involve your username and game stats.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as it is necessary to provide our services. If you request to delete your account, we will remove all personally identifiable information within a reasonable time frame.

Non-personally identifiable data may be retained for analytical purposes.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to inaccurate data.
  • Request the deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of data collection where applicable.
